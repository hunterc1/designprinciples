How Can We Interpret Emphasis?

Emphasis is also known as Focal Point. The main idea, or picture, is know as the focal point or emphasis. This is where you want to put your main point, because this is what viewers will notice first.


Emphasis is important in real world practices because we want people to notice our work. If the emphasis is good on your main point, people will see it, get distracted by it, and then potentially look at everything else you want them to see.
