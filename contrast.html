What is Contrast?

Contrast is an important principle for designing interesting (in contrast to dull) layouts. Contrast, as a principle, offers a great deal of flexibility. There are limitless ways to achieve it.(p.51)

How Can We Interpret Contrast?

Contrast can make an item POP! Contrast is what keeps you interested without being bored. If viewers get bored they will not want to continue with your content.


Contrast is important because if you go to make a flier for your company, and it has no contrast, people will not read it. No one wants to read anything that they get bored with, that is why we need contrast.
